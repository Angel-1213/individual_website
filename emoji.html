The ultimate appearance

78% of emoji users are women. In fact, emojis have became one of the most popular ways of communication in the present day, they express feelings, attitudes and lifestyles.

Surprisingly, Unicode's creation on defult is gender-neutral, meaning that they do not specify this emoji into a certain gender. The table of "Emoji with Explicit Gender Appearance" illustrates emojis that are linked into specific genders. From the table, the emojis are seperated into gender of male and female. Each of these labels are appearance based on age, situation and profession.

Here is a visually appealing video about how emoji evolves, have fun!

However, an update from an explict representation to gender-neutral emojis reveals a shocking truth. The change of the gender into genderless emojis manifests that only 1 out of 4 appearances showcase the gender as 'women'. In addition of the reciprocated data, the disparity highlights the oversight of women's data into the emoji system --they are assuming that gender-neutral equals to 'men', whereas women can be only seen as the exclusives.

The standarization for emojis have been improved a lot during the last few years, there is the introduction of new factors like color, sexual gender and age. Nevertheless, different types and versions of electronics are not up to date on the moderization on the input systems.
