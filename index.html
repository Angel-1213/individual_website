The Forgotten Essence

When it comes to the lives of the other half of humanity, there is often nothing but SILENCE.

Just like the picture, the symbolism of the absence of the bags of popcorn emphasizes the neglectance of women. Their surveillance are seen as unimportant where our main intension is to merely collect data, that is in most circumstances FRAGAMENTARY...

Take a minute to think about this question:

How are women being treated unfairly in terms of data collection?

The absence of data for women, that is closely linked into their everyday life, should be marked rather than ignored, empowered rather than occupied. Throughout this webiste, there are topics that you can discover, be surprised, be inspired and be provoked to share your thoughts to the 'Discussion button' or anyone you like! The topics are the absent facts and statistics that should be utterly visible for the public.
